About this Website

The energy.ma data portal was developed by the Metropolitan Area Planning Council (MAPC) as a tool to help communities learn more about their energy consumption across all key sectors, and develop a plan of action to reduce energy consumption.

energy.ma data portal

Find out how much energy your city or town consumes annually.

1. Learn

Get the big picture on how much energy your community consumes on an annual basis across the key sectors of municipal, residential, commercial, and industrial.

2. Analyze

Dive in to granular analyses which is visually presented to show critical statistics like which buildings to pay attention to, or which fuel sources are contributing to a greater carbon footprint.

3. Act

Take advantage of MAPC's Clean Energy Toolkit to get started on projects and strategies that will help your community reduce its energy consumption and pave the way for cleaner fuel sources.
